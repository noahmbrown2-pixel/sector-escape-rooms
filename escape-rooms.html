SECTOR

INFINITE ESCAPE PROTOCOL · 3D

You wake locked in a cluttered, decaying sector cell. The 4-digit door code is split into four single digits, each printed tiny and faint on a piece of furniture in a different corner of the room.

Walk up to dressers, lockers and cabinets — look at them and the drawers/doors slide open by themselves. Crawl to read what's tucked underneath, climb to reach high shelves. Spot all four digits, then enter the code at the door.

DESKTOP  WASD move · MOUSE look (click to connect) · SPACE jump · C crawl · E door · ladder: stand on it + SPACE
MOBILE   left stick move · drag right to look · JUMP / CRAWL / CLIMB buttons · drawers open on their own
