SECTOR

INFINITE ESCAPE PROTOCOL

You wake locked in a decaying sector cell. A 4-digit access code is hidden somewhere in the room — taped under a bed, stashed inside a locker, or stuck on top of a tall shelf.

Search the furniture. Crawl to look underneath things. Climb the ladder or jump to see what's hidden up high. Find the code, enter it at the door, and descend deeper. Forever.

DESKTOP  WASD move · MOUSE look (click to lock) · SPACE jump · C crawl · E search · climb: stand on ladder + SPACE
MOBILE   left stick move · drag right to look · on-screen JUMP / CRAWL / CLIMB / SEARCH
