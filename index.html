SECTOR

INFINITE ESCAPE PROTOCOL · 3D

You wake locked in a decaying sector cell built from real, solid structures. A 4-digit access code is hidden somewhere — taped under a bed, stashed inside a locker, or stuck on top of a tall shelf.

Search the furniture. Crawl to look underneath. Climb the ladder or jump to reach what's hidden up high. Find the code, enter it at the door, descend deeper. Forever.

DESKTOP  WASD move · MOUSE look (click to lock) · SPACE jump · C crawl · E search · ladder: stand on it + SPACE
MOBILE   left stick move · drag right to look · JUMP / CRAWL / CLIMB / SEARCH buttons
